About DJV2

DJV2 is a solo project aimed at providing retro gaming enthusiasts with an easy way to enjoy classic DOS-based games. With seamless DOSBox integration, DJV2 takes care of all the setup and configurations, making gaming straightforward for users of all experience levels.

Purpose of DJV2

DJV2 was created to simplify the process of playing DOS-based games on modern hardware. Initially developed to help my mom enjoy nostalgic games, DJV2 has grown into a broader tool that aims to bring the joy of classic games to everyone, free from technical barriers.

Key Features

  • High Compatibility: Runs DOS-based games on modern systems with the help of DOSBox, ensuring smooth compatibility.
  • Customizable Settings: Modify graphics, audio, and control settings easily to optimize your gaming experience.
  • Intuitive User Interface: A user-friendly interface that allows even non-tech-savvy users to play games with ease.
  • Save State Management: Save and load games with a single click, making it easy to continue your retro adventures at any time.
  • Automatic Updates: Keep DJV2 up to date with automatic updates that add new features and enhancements.

Frequently Asked Questions

Which platforms are compatible with DJV2?
DJV2 is currently compatible with Windows. Linux and Mac support are under consideration for future releases.
How do I update DJV2?
DJV2 includes a built-in updater that allows you to easily apply updates. Simply click the 'Check for Updates' button in the settings menu.
What games does DJV2 support?
DJV2 is optimized for DOS-based games via DOSBox. It does not support modern games but aims to make retro DOS games as enjoyable as possible.
Is DJV2 open-source?
Currently, DJV2 is not open-source, but we welcome community feedback and suggestions for future improvements.
