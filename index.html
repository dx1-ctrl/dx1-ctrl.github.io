About DJV2

DJV2 is designed to make playing retro DOS games as simple as possible. This solo project focuses on creating a seamless, modern interface for launching and managing classic games on DOSBox, bridging the gap between past and present gaming.

Purpose of DJV2

The original goal was to help my mom enjoy nostalgic games with ease. Since then, DJV2 has evolved into a user-friendly tool that allows anyone to dive into retro gaming without hassle.

Key Features

High Compatibility

DJV2 seamlessly runs DOS games on modern hardware with DOSBox integration, ensuring smooth gameplay and high compatibility.

Customizable Settings

Modify graphics, sound, and controls to suit your preferences for the best retro gaming experience.

Save State Management

Save and load game states anytime, making it easy to pick up where you left off in your classic adventures.
