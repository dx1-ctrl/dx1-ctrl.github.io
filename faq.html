Frequently Asked Questions

Which platforms are compatible with DJV2?
DJV2 is currently compatible with Windows. Linux and Mac support are under consideration for future releases.
How do I update DJV2?
DJV2 includes a built-in updater. Simply open the app, and it will notify you of any available updates, which can be installed with a single click.
What types of games are supported?
DJV2 is optimized for DOS-based games, making it ideal for classic titles. Modern games are not supported, as DJV2 is designed specifically for retro gaming enthusiasts.
Can I customize controls and graphics?
Yes! DJV2 provides a range of customization options for controls, graphics, and audio. This allows you to tailor the gameplay experience to suit your preferences and hardware setup.
Is DJV2 free to use?
Yes, DJV2 is free to download and use. There are no subscription fees or hidden costs, so you can enjoy retro gaming without any financial commitment.
Does DJV2 support multiplayer functionality?
Currently, DJV2 does not support multiplayer functionality. Our focus is on enhancing single-player experiences for classic DOS games, though multiplayer may be considered in future updates.
