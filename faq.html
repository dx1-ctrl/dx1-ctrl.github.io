Frequently Asked Questions

Which platforms are compatible with DJV2?
DJV2 is compatible with Windows. Support for Linux and Mac systems is under consideration for future releases.
How do I update DJV2?
DJV2 has a built-in updater. Simply open the app, and it will notify you of any available updates, which you can install with a single click.
What types of games are supported?
DJV2 is optimized for DOS-based games, making it ideal for classic titles from the DOS era. Modern games are not supported, as DJV2 is specifically designed for retro gaming enthusiasts.
Can I customize controls and graphics?
Yes, DJV2 offers a range of customization options for controls, graphics, and audio. This allows you to tailor the gameplay experience to suit your preferences and hardware setup.
Is DJV2 free to use?
Yes, DJV2 is free to download and use. There are no subscription fees or hidden costs, so you can enjoy retro gaming without any financial commitment.
Does DJV2 support multiplayer functionality?
Currently, DJV2 does not support multiplayer functionality. Our focus is on enhancing single-player experiences for classic DOS games, though multiplayer may be considered in future updates.
How can I organize my game library?
DJV2 includes a library management feature that allows you to organize, add, remove, and categorize your games. This makes it easy to keep track of your collection and find your favorites.
Is DJV2 difficult to set up for beginners?
Not at all! DJV2 was designed with simplicity in mind. The interface is intuitive, and the setup process is easy to follow, making it accessible for users of all experience levels.
Can I map my own keys for controls?
Absolutely! DJV2 includes custom key mapping, so you can set controls that are comfortable for you, ensuring an enjoyable gaming experience.
